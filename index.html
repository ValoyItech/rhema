About Us

Rhema Funeral Services is dedicated to providing compassionate, respectful, and affordable funeral arrangements to our community. We understand the difficulty of saying goodbye and strive to make this process as smooth and dignified as possible.

With years of experience in the funeral industry, our team is committed to honoring your loved ones with the care and respect they deserve.

Terms and Conditions

Membership

Joining is free. Each funeral policy can cover up to 14 beneficiaries, plus 1 policy holder (the person responsible for payments).

We don't allow casket and tombstone from other funeral parlours.

Penalties

  • We will charge a R200 fee for a missed premium and R400 fee for two missed premiums when you have a funeral that is not conducted by us.
  • We will charge you a penalty fee of R1500 for a missed premium and R2500 penalty fee for two missed premiums when we conduct the funeral service.
  • We will charge a penalty fee of R6000 for three missed premiums.
  • Your policy will lapse after four unpaid premiums.

Funeral Arrangements

We don't do body removal from other funerals mortuary.

Saturday funerals are arranged till Wednesday at 12:00. Any request after 12:00 will be for Sunday or the following weekend.

Deceased Repatriation

We offer free Repatriation services in Limpopo and Gauteng provinces.

For Repatriation in other provinces within South Africa, we make special arrangements to ensure your loved one is returned to their final resting place with dignity and respect.
