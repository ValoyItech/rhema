About Us

Rhema Funeral Services is dedicated to providing compassionate, respectful, and affordable funeral arrangements to our community. We understand the difficulty of saying goodbye and strive to make this process as smooth and dignified as possible.

With years of experience in the funeral industry, our team is committed to honoring your loved ones with the care and respect they deserve.

Our Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and respected funeral service provider in our region and beyond.

We aspire to set the standard of excellence in funeral care by continuously innovating our services, embracing diversity, and treating every family with compassion, empathy, and cultural sensitivity.

Terms and Conditions

Deceased Pickup

  • R200 if passed away at home
  • R400 if passed away at hospital or clinic
  • R1500 if passed away elsewhere or R2500 if two deaths

Funeral Arrangements

Must be made at the funeral parlour. Only one coffin provided per funeral unless upgraded.

Funeral services must start before 12:00 noon. If service starts after 12:00, it will be treated as a Sunday service with an extra fee.

Membership

Joining is free. Maintain a minimum of 14 people per group. After 15 members, you get a free book.

Members must attend society meetings whenever necessary. If a society does not attend meetings, Rhema Funeral Services is not responsible for missed dates.

Penalties

R6000 fine for missing monthly payments.

Replacement fee required if membership booklet is lost.
